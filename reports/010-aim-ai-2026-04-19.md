## C) Level and Strategy

**Detected role level**
- Broadly junior-to-mid, but with startup ownership expectations.
- They are not filtering on years. They are filtering on evidence of shipping.

**Your natural level for this archetype**
- Junior-mid AI engineer with unusually strong production-agent depth for experience level.
- Credible for the role if positioned as an execution-heavy builder rather than a polished enterprise consultant.

**Sell senior without lying**
- Lead with production proof, not with titles.
- Emphasize that you already build systems that move from idea to live workflows: agents, APIs, retrieval, evaluation, CI/CD.
- Use the founder-mode framing lightly: fast ramp, low ego, high ownership, comfortable with ambiguity.
- Say explicitly that your edge is shipping practical AI systems end-to-end, then tightening them with metrics.

**If they try to downlevel**
- Accept only if the scope still includes real deployment ownership.
- Ask for explicit expectations for the first 90 days.
- Ask how success is measured after launch: uptime, conversion, call quality, integration speed, customer expansion.
- If comp is soft, negotiate for a formal 6-month review tied to expanded ownership.
